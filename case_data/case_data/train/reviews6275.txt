Valerian is an incredible visual treat

I've seen all the sci-fi/action/adventure movies out there and I can
tell that Luc Besson's Valerian is doing way better than most of them.Valerian is an incredible visual treat, the best you've seen since
Avatar. There are all the ingredients to make a great Sci-fi movie
saga.Luc Besson is doing his Sci-Fi movies better & better. The future of
French Sci-Fi films is very promising. Let's hope this will continue.
