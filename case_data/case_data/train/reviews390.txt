The movie is a romantic comedy cliché, but Allison Brie looks hot

A rather standard romantic comedy, filled with clichés (like the sassy
BFF, which is again played by Natasha Leon, just like the character she
played in American Pie) and the usual cynical, fast and supposedly
witty dialogue (no one actually speaks like that, not even in NY).
However, Allison Brie has a gorgeous body and is often seen wearing
sexy revealing outfits, which is great.
